Before you flash
1
Enter bootloader mode
Hold the BOOT button (left button on the front of the device), unplug and replug the USB cable, then release BOOT. The screen will go blank — the device is ready.
Skip if the device has never been flashed.
2
Click Connect & Flash below
A port picker will appear — select your T-Display (usually labelled CP210x or Silicon Labs).
3
Wait ~30 seconds
Do not unplug during flashing. The device restarts automatically into the Atlantis DeepSea setup screen.
